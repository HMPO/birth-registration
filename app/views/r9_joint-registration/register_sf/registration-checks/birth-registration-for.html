Before you start

What you need to do

Part 1: Review information provided by the mother

Check the details the mother provided for the registration:

  • mother’s details (such as name, place of birth)
  • child’s details (such as name, date of birth)

You need to discuss any changes directly with the mother.

While you review, the mother cannot change this information, or register the birth with your details.

Part 2: Provide father's or parent's details for registration

Including name, place of birth, and job role.

The mother must confirm they agree to register the birth with the details you provided.

Part 3: Answer questions for government services

For example, your date of birth and employment status.

Part 4: Final review of details

You and the mother will both need to:

  • review all the details
  • confirm they agree to register with these details
  • discuss any changes outside the service

After you both confirm you agree, the mother can submit the information and order birth certificates.

The mother cannot change any details - or provide your information for the registration - without your agreement.

Saving your answers

You can save answers after each section if you need to stop and continue later.
