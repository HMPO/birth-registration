Invitation to join a birth registration

About this invitation

The child’s mother starts the online process for a birth registration.

This invitation is for the child’s father or parent to join the registration.

We cannot show any details about the mother or the child until you verify your identity.

About birth registration

Registration is the official record of someone’s birth including:

  • child’s name, date and place of birth
  • mother’s name, place of birth, and job role
  • {father/parent}’s name, place of birth and job role

You can order birth certifcates after the registration. Birth certificates can be used to access services, for example to prove the birth took place or your relationship to the child.

Providing information

The mother has started the process, and entered some information.

You’ll need to:

  • check the information provided by the mother - the child’s and mother’s details
  • provide your own details for the father or parent details
  • confirm you agree to register with the details provided

You cannot submit the completed information unless both parents agree to all the information in the registration.
