Getting birth certificates

You can get birth certificates:

  • immediately if you register in person
  • by post if you register online

Online registration

You can order copies of the birth certificate as part of the online service.

They cost:

  • £12 for standard delivery (around 12 working days)
  • £38 for next day delivery
  • £11 to collect in person

Delivery estimates are from the time the certificates are sent.

In-person registration

You can buy certificates at the appointment.

They cost £12 each.

You can buy extra copies of the birth certificate from the register office at any time.
