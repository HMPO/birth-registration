Register a birth in England or Wales

You’ll need to register a birth within 42 days (6 weeks).

You can provide the information online or in person.

A register office will contact you if do not register a birth in this time.

How to register

How you register - including what documents to provide - are different depending on who is being registered as parents.

Births outside England and Wales

The process is different for children born in Scotland, Northern Ireland, or outside the UK.
