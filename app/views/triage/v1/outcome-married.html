Register the birth online or in person

Registering online

If you register online:

  • one parent can complete the registration
  • a local register office will check the information
  • birth certificates are sent by post
  • you’ll verify your identity with GOV.UK One Login
  • you’ll need your child’s NHS number

Registering in person

If you register in person:

  • you’ll visit the register office in the area your child was born
  • you must book an appointment
  • staff will record your details
  • only one parent needs to attend
  • you’ll get the birth certificate at the appointment
