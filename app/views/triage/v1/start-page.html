Births in England and Wales must be registered (online or in-person) within 42 days.

After a birth registration, you can apply for government services, such as child benefit or your child’s passport.

What you need to provide

To register you’ll give:

  • child’s name, date of birth and NHS number
  • birth mother’s details
  • father (or female parent) details
  • proof of identification (a document or online with GOV.UK One Login)

How to register

Depending on your situation, you may need to provide other documents.
