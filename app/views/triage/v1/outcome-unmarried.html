Register the birth online or in person

Registering online

If you register online:

  • either parent can start the registration - the other parent will sign in separately to review
  • both parents prove their identities with GOV.UK One Login
  • registrations are checked by a local register office
  • birth certificates are sent by post
  • you’ll need your child’s NHS number

Registering in person

If you register in person:

  • both parents must attend the local register office
  • you must book an appointment
  • staff will enter the information about both parents and your child
  • you’ll get birth certificate at the appointment
