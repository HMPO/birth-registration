Before you submit

Final check of birth registration details

You have completed all the information for birth registrations.

Next, you need to:

  • check details provided by father/parent
  • submit details to register office
  • order birth certificates (optional)

Birth certificates

Birth certificates are £12.50 each, including delivery by post. The register office for the area you are born in may also offer collection.

You can order copies as part of the service. Or order copies any time after the registration.

There is no fee for registration.
