Confirmation: details submitted for a birth registration

Hello Jane Smith,

You’ve entered details to register the birth of your child.

In a separate email, we’ve sent an invite link to share with the child’s {father/parent}.

You will submit the final registration details to the register office after the {father/parent} has:

  • agreed to register the birth with the details you provided, such as child’s name
  • add added their own details (such as name and job title)

The invite link expires after 7 days.

Choosing to invite the {father/parent}

You and your child’s {father/parent} must jointly agree on all the information in the birth registration.

Parents recorded on a birth registration usually have the right to:

  • make decisions about the child’s upbringing
  • certain information about the child

If {father/parent} requests changes

 {Father/Parent} might request changes to the information, such as fixing a mistake or adding missing information.

Both you and the father must agree on all the information before you can submit the registration to the register office.
