Updates ready to review by father or parent

The father or parent can now sign in to review the updated details.

They must:

  • agree to register the birth with the updated details
  • add their own details (such as name and job title)

You'll need to:

  • wait for the notification the father or parent has completed the information
  • submit the final details to the register office
  • order any birth certificates

If your situation changes

Sign in to the service if you need to register without the father or parent, or delete this application.

Guidance and support

Read our guidance about registering a birth, such as getting certificates and how long it takes.

Contact the Register Office if you need a registration more urgently or your situation changes.
