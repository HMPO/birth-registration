Child's name

This will be the full name, recorded on the birth certificate.

Help with name

Changing or adding a name after registration is a separate application process. You will usually pay a fee and need to provide evidence.

You cannot record names on an official documents that are misleading, offensive or impossible to pronounce by a human.

For example, Aimee-Lou not Aimee Lou
Do not include titles such as ‘Mr’ or ‘Miss’. Only add legally inherited titles, such as ‘Lady’ or ‘Lord’
For example ‘Junior’ or ‘III’
