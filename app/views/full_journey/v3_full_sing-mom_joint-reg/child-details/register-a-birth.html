Register a birth

What you need to provide

Part 1: Child’s details

Including name, place and date of birth, and gender.

Part 2: Mother’s details.

Including name, place of birth, job role, and previous names.

Part 3: Questions for government services

For example, your date of birth and employment status.

Saving your answers

You can save answers after each section if you need to stop and continue later.

Entering the {father/parent}’s details

You’ll get a unique invite link to send to the {father/parent}.

They’ll use the link to:

  • agree to be part of the registration
  • add their own details, such as name, place of birth

Afterwards, you’ll use the service to confirm registration is ready to submit to the register office.

Birth certificates

You can order birth certificates after the {father/parent} provides their details.
