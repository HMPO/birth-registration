Part 1 of 4: Child’s details

Child's name

This will be the name recorded on official documents.

Help with name

The child cannot be given a name that is any of the following:

  1. spelt in numerals
  2. impossible to say out loud in any spoken language
  3. offensive
  4. misleading (for example, adding a title/prefix such as ‘Duke’ or ‘Baroness’)
For example, Aimee-Lou not Aimee Lou
Do not include titles such as ‘Mr’ or Miss’. Only add legally inherited titles, such as ‘Lady’ or ‘Lord’
For example ‘Junior’ or ‘III’
