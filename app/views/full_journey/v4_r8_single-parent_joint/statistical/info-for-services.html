Questions for government services

Part of registration is providing information for government statistics and reporting.

Each of the following questions will explain:

  • what information is being collected and why
  • what service will use the information
  • if you are required to answer by law

Your answers do not affect the registration.

! Warning By law you must provide this information if you know it. You can be fined up to £200 if you do not.

Your privacy

Your answers are separate from personal information you gave for the registration.
