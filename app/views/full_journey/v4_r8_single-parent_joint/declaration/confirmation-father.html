Sent to mother for final check

You’ll get a confirmation email or text.

What happens next

The mother will:

  • check all the information in the registration
  • agree to register the birth with the details you provided
  • send the completed details to the register office
  • order any birth certificates

You get an email notification when the application is submitted.

If the mother requests a change

You and the mother must jointly agree on all information entered in the birth registration.

Discuss any changes with the mother before you continue the registration.

Getting birth certificate

If you need birth certificate later you can order a birth certificate online after registration is complete.
