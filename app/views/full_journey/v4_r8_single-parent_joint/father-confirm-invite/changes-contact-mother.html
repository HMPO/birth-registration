Request a change from the mother

You must contact the mother to discuss:

  • information they provided
  • any mistakes or errors

You cannot discuss changes through the online service.

The mother will be able to update the information provided for the Mother’s and Child’s details.

What happens next

The mother must ask you to review the information again - whether or not the information has changed.

You’ll get a notification once the mother sends it for review again.

You cannot be recorded as the father unless both you and the mother agree on all the information in the registration.
