Before you submit

Check your answers

Check the answers you’ve provided. Make any changes or correct any mistakes or errors.

Mistakes or errors can be changed after the registration - there's usually a fee.

Make a declaration

Digitally make a statement that that you’ve completed the registration honestly.

Order copies of the birth certificate (optional)

You can order certificates after you submit. They are £12.50 each, including delivery by post.

You can order copies at any time after the registration.

You must have a birth certificate to apply for the child’s first passport and any child benefits, as well as other services, such a GP.
