Getting the birth certificate

You’ll need to order copies of the birth certificate, if you need one. You can also order them after the registration is approved.

Sometimes called ‘certified copy’.

We send them by post when the registration is approved.

You do not pay a fee for the registration.

Do you want to order certificates?
Copies of the certificate are used to apply for the child’s first passport and other government services
Certificates are £12.50 for each copy
You’ll get a receipt to confirm that the birth has been registered
