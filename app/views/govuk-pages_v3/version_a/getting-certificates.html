Getting birth certificates

You can get birth certificates for your child:

  • immediately after you register a birth in person
  • by post - order as part of online registration or any time after that

Online registration

You can order copies of the birth certificate as part of the online service.

They cost £12.50 for standard delivery (around 12 working days).

Delivery estimates are from the time the certificates are sent.

In-person registration

You can buy certificates at the appointment.

They cost £12.50 each.

Any time after registration

You can buy extra copies of the birth certificate online or from a local Register Office or Local Authority at any time.

They cost £12.50 each.
