Register a birth:  opposite-sex parents  (married or in a civil partnership)

Married parents can choose to:

  • register online - one parent can complete the online service
  • register in-person at a register office - only one parent needs to attend

Registering online

One parent can complete the registration online, including ordering birth certificates.

Your identity will be checked by GOV.UK One Login. This works in a similar way to Government Gateway.

After you submit the form, a registrar at the council will complete the official registration.

Birth certificates will be sent by post. They cost £12.50 each (standard delivery).

In person registration

You’ll make an appointment at the register office for the area your child was born.

Only one parent is required to attend the appointment. You do not need to take your child.

You’ll get birth certificates at the appointment. Each birth certificate costs £12.50.
