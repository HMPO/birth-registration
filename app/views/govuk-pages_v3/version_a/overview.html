What is registration

Births in England and Wales must be registered within 42 days (6 weeks). After 6 weeks the local Register Office or Local Authority may contact you.

Registration is the official record of someone’s birth. Afterwards you can order birth certificates to apply for government services.

Information you provide

To register you’ll provide:

  • child’s NHS number, name, date, and place of birth
  • birth mother’s name, place of birth, and job role
  • details of the father or birth mother’s female partner (if applicable)

How to register

How you register - including what documents to provide - are different depending on who is being registered as parents.
