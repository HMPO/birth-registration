Registering only the mother

In UK legislation, the ‘mother’ is the person who gave birth to the child.

It’s your choice to register only your own details if you are the child’s mother.

A father or parent can be recorded after the initial registration, if your situation changes.

You can either:

  • register online
  • register in person at Register Office or Local Authority
